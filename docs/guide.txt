Game Goal: Collect coins and avoid oncoming aircrafts.

1. Game Controls - - - 
The game is played on a 3-lane track and requires you to move your jet left and right to dodge obstacles and collect items.

Platform	Action	 Controls
Desktop	Left	 A key or Left Arrow key
Desktop	Right         D key or Right Arrow key
Mobile	Left	Tap the Left side of the screen or Swipe Left
Mobile	Right	Tap the Right side of the screen or Swipe Right
In-Game	Pause       Click the Pause Button in the top left

2. Core Gameplay Mechanics - - - 
Score: Measured in meters (m). It increases based on your effective game speed.

This shop uses your Total Coins to purchase permanent upgrades and items.
